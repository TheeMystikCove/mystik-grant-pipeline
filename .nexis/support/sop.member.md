# Grant Engine — Member Support Guide

*Access tier: MEMBER (Tier 4). This SOP is visible to all members accessing the Grant Engine.*

---

## I. What Is the Grant Engine?

The Grant Engine is Thee Mystik Universal Holdings Corp.'s grant research and pipeline workspace. Members who have been granted access use it to:

- Explore active and eligible grant opportunities
- Review grant research summaries and scoring notes
- Track the status of submitted applications
- Access grant document templates and preparation guides

If you are not sure whether you are eligible for Grant Engine access, contact HQ staff support.

---

## II. Common Member Questions

### "I can't find the grant I was told about."

1. Confirm the grant name or reference ID (check any email or message you received)
2. Use the search or filter tools in the Grant Engine dashboard
3. Confirm your account is fully activated — pending accounts may not see all opportunities
4. If the grant is not visible after confirming the above, submit a support request

### "My grant status hasn't updated."

1. Refresh the page and allow up to 60 seconds for sync
2. Check the timeline tab on the grant record — updates may be pending review by an admin
3. Status changes are logged — if you believe an error occurred, note the grant ID and current status before submitting a support request

### "I don't have access to a document or template."

1. Confirm you are logged in with the correct account
2. Some documents are only released after certain pipeline stages are complete
3. If you believe you should have access at your current stage, submit a support request with the document name and grant record ID

### "I received an error message."

1. Note the full error text and the page you were on
2. Take a screenshot if possible
3. Refresh and try again once — some errors are transient
4. If the error persists, submit a support request with the error text

---

## III. What NEXIS Can Help With

- Explaining what grant stages mean and what each status indicates
- Directing you to the right document or template
- Walking you through a support request step by step
- Clarifying terminology in grant descriptions or scoring criteria

## IV. What NEXIS Cannot Do

- Submit or modify grant applications on your behalf
- Make eligibility determinations — those are made by the grants team
- Access backend pipeline data
- Override document access restrictions

---

## V. Submitting a Support Request

If NEXIS cannot resolve your issue:

1. Note your MYSK-ID and the grant record ID involved
2. Describe the issue clearly: what you expected, what happened, what you have already tried
3. Submit via the Grant Engine support channel or contact HQ staff support directly

**Response target:** Same business day
